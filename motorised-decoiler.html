Overview

Our company is a reputed supplier, exporter and manufacturer of Decoiler. Our range is fabricated by our highly skilled and assiduous processionals in adherence with international standards using finest quality raw material which is acquired from renowned dealers. This product is an essential part of fluent operation and comes with various features to fulfill all the requirements of customers.Under the expert guidance and able supervision of skilled and experienced workforce, we are engrossed in exporting, manufacturing and supplying an extensive range of Motorised Decoiler.

Features

All motorised decoilers are chain driven soft start & stop through mechanical torque limiter & friction drag brake.

  • Rigid Fabricated Structure
  • Drive System : A.C. Fixed Speed /A.C. Variable Speed (optional)
  • Three / Four Self Centering Coil-location jaws with mechanical gear reducer for models above 2.5 Tonne
  • All motorized decoilers are chain driven soft start & stop through mechanical torque limiter & friction drag brake
